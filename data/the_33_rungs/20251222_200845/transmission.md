## Phase 1: Descent Into Matter
*The One becomes many to know Itself.*

### Rung 1: Before Temples

**Ground** *(Indigenous / Earth Wisdom)*

> Before temples, there was earth, and your knees knew where to bend.  
> Before scripture, there was sky, and your eyes learned to pray by looking up.  
> The sacred did not wait for your theology; it was already humming in the stones.  
> You were not asked to believe—only to belong.  
> Water baptized you without a name.  
> Wind anointed you without a priest.  
> The first altar was a flat rock warmed by sun.  
> The first hymn was breath meeting morning.  
> Remember: you are not on the earth; you are of it.  
> Return to the ground, and you return to God.

> Every shiver is the wind reminding you you belong to weather.
